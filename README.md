# Aventris Capital

**Wealth Wellness Hub for the modern investor.**

Aventris Capital helps investors understand the true health and resilience of their financial portfolios by consolidating assets into a single intelligent dashboard.

---

## What It Does

Instead of focusing on stock picking or return prediction, Aventris aggregates traditional and digital assets and analyzes them through the lens of financial wellness — giving you a clear picture of how your wealth is actually structured.

**Supported asset types:**
- Equities & ETFs
- Cryptocurrency
- Cash
- Private holdings

---

## Core Features

- **Wealth Health Score** — A single, digestible score that reflects the overall resilience of your portfolio
- **Diversification Analysis** — Identify hidden concentrations across sectors, asset classes, and geographies
- **Liquidity Metrics** — Understand how accessible your wealth actually is
- **Market Correlation** — See how exposed your portfolio is to broader macro movements
- **Stress-Test Scenarios** — Simulate market downturns to reveal vulnerabilities before they become losses
- **Visual Breakdowns** — Clear charts and insights that replace complex spreadsheets

---

## Who It's For

Aventris is built for **retail investors and emerging wealth builders** who want more than just returns — they want to understand the true structure of their wealth and make more informed, resilient financial decisions.

---

## Philosophy

Most financial tools tell you what your portfolio is worth. Aventris tells you how healthy it is.

By transforming fragmented financial data into clear, actionable insights, Aventris empowers everyday investors to move from reactive decision-making to proactive financial wellness.

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Node.js
- **Data:** Yahoo Finance API
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Drizzle ORM)
